Ham Egg and Cheese Sandwich

A simple sandwich made from common, easily obtainable ingredients.

    Ingredients
  • Two slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Deli sliced ham
  • Your choice of cheese
  • One egg
  • Sriracha
  • Mayonnaise
  • vegetable oil
    Directions
  1. Put vegetable oil in a pan and let it heat
  2. While the pan heats, mix sriracha and mayonnaise. Set aside for later
  3. Butter one side each slice of bread
  4. By this time, the pan should be hot enough. Crack an egg into the time (optional: use a spatula or other cooking utensil to shape the egg into about the same size as your bread
  5. While the egg is cooking, put your sauce on one of your slices of bread. Then, layer the ham onto the bread, followed by the cheese you selected. Make sure the ingredients inside the sandwich are on the NON-buttered side. When the egg is done, the heat will help melt the cheese!
  6. When the egg finishes cooking, add it the the sandwich. Close the sandwich and put it onto the pan with one of the buttered sides down.
  7. When one side is golden brown, flip the sandwich so the other side can also brown up
  8. When both sides are done, remove from the pan and enjoy!
